About Onette 
Onette is a love letter to Schöneberg, founded by friends Tamara and Brie. We blend the nostalgia of New York luncheonettes with the cozy soul of a Berlin corner pub. 
In our corner spot on Grunewaldstraße, we serve elevated comfort food, think crispy fish buns, seasonal pies, and fresh salads.
